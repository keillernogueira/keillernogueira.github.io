Welcome!

I am a Lecturer and part of the Machine Learning Research Group within the School of Computer Science and Informatics at the University of Liverpool, UK. I received my PhD in Computer Science from the Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais (UFMG), Brazil, with part of my doctoral research conducted at GIPSA-Lab, Institut Polytechnique de Grenoble, France.

My research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, deep learning, pattern recognition, image processing, and remote sensing.

Research

My goal is to develop methods that enable robots to see and act like humans, spanning computer vision, machine learning, and robotics.

✋
Affordance Detection & Grasping
We develop methods for object affordance detection and language-driven grasping. Our Grasp-Anything dataset enables large-scale language-conditioned grasp research. Recent work focuses on 6-DoF grasping with negative prompt guidance and mask-guided attention.
IROS'16IROS'17ICRA'18IROS'23CVPR'24ECCV'24 OralIROS'24
🌐
Foundation & Generative Models
We utilise foundation models for open-vocabulary tasks and develop VLMs for robotic applications (Robotic-CLIP). We propose generative techniques for scene synthesis and music-driven dance generation.
NeurIPS'23CVPR'23SIGGRAPH Asia'23ECCV'24ICRA'25
⚡
Efficient AI
Real-time inference is critical for robotics, medical, and manufacturing applications. We develop lightweight models for autonomous driving, medical imaging, robotic grasping, and federated learning.
IROS'20TMI'22IV'22Mechatronics'23ICCV'23IROS'24
🏥
Autonomous Catheterisation
We lead research in developing a world-first autonomous catheterisation robot. We also developed CathSim, a realistic open-source simulator for endovascular intervention, and large-scale datasets (CathAction, Guide3D).
ICRA'20TBME'21TBME'22T-MRB'24IROS'25
🔬
Medical Imaging
We tackle challenging problems in medical imaging including depth estimation, medical VQA, deformable registration, sensing area detection, and retinal image analysis.
MICCAI'21MICCAI'22TMI'22MICCAI'23MICCAI'24
🌍
Federated Learning
Collecting centralised data violates user privacy. We propose federated learning techniques for autonomous driving, non-IID data handling, topology design, and distributed medical imaging.
IV'22ICCV'23ICRA'24MICCAI'23IROS'25
